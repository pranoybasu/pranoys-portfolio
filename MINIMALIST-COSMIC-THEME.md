## Minimalist Animation Principles

### DO Use:
âœ… Fade in/out transitions (300-500ms)
âœ… Gentle translate/scale on hover (1.02x max)
âœ… Smooth scroll reveals (intersection observer)
âœ… Subtle underline animations
âœ… Clean modal/dialog transitions
âœ… Progress indicators
âœ… Skeleton loaders for content

### DON'T Use:
âŒ Constant floating/bobbing animations
âŒ Heavy particle systems everywhere
âŒ Physics simulations on every element
âŒ Excessive glow effects
âŒ Complex 3D transformations
âŒ Distracting cursor trails

### Animation Timing:
- **Fast**: 200ms (micro-interactions)
- **Normal**: 300-500ms (transitions)
- **Slow**: 800ms (section reveals)
- **Easing**: cubic-bezier(0.4, 0.0, 0.2, 1)
